# Revoid

[Itch.io Page](https://shadowscythe-games.itch.io/revoid)

## Introduction
Enter a world of reflex, focus, and endless challenge!  

In this minimalist yet colorful arcade game, you control a glowing circle that races endlessly around a circular track.  
Your mission is simple: survive as long as you can by avoiding deadly spikes.

## Gameplay
Tap the screen at the right moment to move your circle to the opposite side of the track.  
Time your jumps carefully — one mistake and you’ll crash into a spike and have to start over!  

- Each successful dodge earns you a point  
- Your best score is always tracked, pushing you to beat your own record  

While dodging obstacles, you can also collect shiny crystals.  
These crystals unlock a variety of vibrant skins in the shop, letting you customize your circle and brighten the dark background with dazzling colors.

## Features
- Fast-paced reflex gameplay with simple one-tap controls  
- Dark, minimalist background that makes colorful skins pop  
- Endless challenge: dodge spikes and see how high you can score  
- Collect crystals to unlock new skins and customize your circle  
- Track your best score and challenge yourself to beat it  
- Addictive, quick sessions perfect for both short breaks and long play  

## Challenge
How long can you survive the track?  
Test your reflexes, unlock your favorite skins, and enjoy a simple but deeply addictive arcade experience.
